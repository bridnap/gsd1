Welcome to the home of Gooey Goodness


The Little Popper is an exceptional and unique gourmet popcorn company! Our company was started with an idea that popcorn can have a place in the world of decadent deserts, and was meant to be experienced by gourmet popcorn lovers all over the world over! Our family has spent over three years working on our secret recipes, now we are able to spread the love all over the world. YUM!

Our Goal

Our goal is to give you best quality popcorn at a reasonable price, we can customize any type of popcorn you would like and it's all about the customers needs and wants. We work hard to bring you the best! We don't settle for anything less than top quality ingredients and top quality workmanship.

The Little Popper Gourmet Popcorn, is centered around hometown values, community spirit and neighborly love. We can help you with fundraising, corporate parties, birthday parties, weddings, and school events. WE all love popcorn so it will be a hit wherever we cater!

Love at first bite!

Make sure you taste some of our best flavors like gourmet cheddar popcorn, cookies and cream, and our wonderful Snicker flavors to start!
